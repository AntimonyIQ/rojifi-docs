## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our systems seriously, and we value the security community. The disclosure of security vulnerabilities helps us ensure the security and privacy of our users.

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability in one of our products or platforms please send it to us by emailing security@rojifi.com.

Please include the following details with your report:

*   Description of the location and potential impact of the vulnerability;
*   A detailed description of the steps required to reproduce the vulnerability (POC scripts, screenshots, and compressed screen captures are all helpful to us); and
*   Your name/handle and a link for recognition in our Hall of Fame.

We ask that you do not use vulnerabilities against our services, including:

*   Accessing or modifying other users' data;
*   Causing a denial of service (DoS); or
*   Any form of social engineering.

## Response Timeline

We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 7 business days and strive to send you regular updates about our progress. If the vulnerability is accepted, we will coordinate the disclosure timeline with you.

## Scope

The following domains are in scope for our bug bounty program:

*   *.rojifi.com
*   api.rojifi.com

Only test on accounts you own or have explicit permission to test on.

## Out of Scope

*   Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)
*   Self-XSS
*   Spamming

Thank you for helping keep Rojifi and our users safe!
